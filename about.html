NUS AI and Data Science and Informatics in Astronomy

NADIA

The NADIA group combines artificial intelligence and data science with astronomy to discover new frontiers in stellar astrophysics and exoplanet science.

Our Mission

To revolutionize our understanding of stars and planetary systems through innovative applications of machine learning and data science to astronomical datasets.

We develop novel computational methods to extract maximum scientific value from current and next-generation astronomical surveys, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in stellar and exoplanetary science.

Research Interests

Asteroseismology

Listening to the sounds of stars to reveal their interiors.

Exoplanetary Science

Detection and characterization of exoplanets and their atmospheres, using missions like JWST, Roman.

Galactic Archeology

Piecing together the history of our galaxy with chemical, kinematic, and seismic data.

Data-Driven Stellar Modelling

Characterizing and mitigating stellar activity signals that complicate exoplanet detection and characterization.

Time-Domain Machine Learning

Pioneering applications of convolutional neural networks, transformers, and other architectures to time domain astronomy.

Anomaly Detection

Developing scalable methods to process and analyze the enormous datasets from current and future sky surveys for rogue objects and weird phenomena.
