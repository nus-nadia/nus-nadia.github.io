Our Outreach Programs

School Visits

Our researchers visit local schools to give talks and hands-on workshops about astronomy and data science.

Public Lectures

Regular talks at science festivals and public venues to share exciting discoveries in astronomy and AI.

Citizen Science

Projects that allow the public to contribute to real astronomical research by analyzing data.

Teacher Workshops

Professional development programs to help educators bring astronomy and data science into classrooms.

Science Festivals

Interactive exhibits and demonstrations at major science festivals and public events.

Online Resources

Educational videos, tutorials, and open-source tools for learning about astronomy and data science.
