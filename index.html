Research Focus Areas

Astroseismology

Using stellar oscillations to probe the internal structure and evolution of stars through advanced signal processing and machine learning techniques.

Exoplanet Detection

Developing novel algorithms to identify and characterize exoplanets from large astronomical datasets, pushing the boundaries of discovery.

AI in Astronomy

Pioneering the application of deep learning and other AI methods to solve complex problems in modern astronomy and astrophysics.
